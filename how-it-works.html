How cheapSkate works

The complete breakdown of how the extension, affiliate system, and cashback payouts work together.

01

You install the extension

One-click install from the Chrome Web Store (or load from GitHub for early access). No account required to start browsing.

When you first visit a supported merchant, cheapSkate prompts you to create an account with your email. This assigns your user ID and enables payout tracking.

02

Extension syncs our offer database

cheapSkate pulls available deals and cashback rates from our server every 30 minutes. This data comes from affiliate networks we've joined — Amazon Associates, CJ, ShareASale, eBay, and more.

When you land on a supported store, the extension checks its local database for available offers.

03

At checkout, we show you the deal

When you reach the checkout page, cheapSkate checks its database and finds the best available offer for that store.

A clean popup appears showing what you can save (e.g., "5% cashback at Nike").

Why at checkout?

This is the moment you're most likely to buy — and the moment you'll benefit most from a discount or cashback offer. We surface the deal right when it matters.

04

You click "Apply & Save"

When you click the offer, you're redirected to the merchant with the deal applied. This is a genuine click-based referral — exactly how Honey, Swagbucks, and TopCashback work.

You land back on the merchant's checkout page with the coupon or cashback ready to use.

05

You complete your purchase

Shop as normal. The affiliate network tracks the sale. When the order is confirmed, they notify cheapSkate with the commission amount.

Our server records the conversion and calculates your share.

06

Cashback hits your account

Your 50% share is credited to your cheapSkate balance. You can see it grow in the extension popup anytime.

Withdrawals are processed in crypto (BTC or USDC) with a $5 minimum. No KYC, no bank forms.

FAQ

Common questions

Is this just cookie stuffing?

No. You actively click to apply the offer. The redirect through our affiliate link is a genuine referral click — the same model used by every major cashback site. Cookie stuffing (silent drops without a click) is against affiliate network ToS. We don't do that.

Do I need to create an account?

You can install and browse without one. When you're ready to earn cashback, you'll create an account with your email so we can track your balance and process payouts.

What data does the extension collect?

cheapSkate sees which domains you visit (to check for offers) and sends conversion data (order amount, merchant) when you complete a purchase. We don't collect browsing history, search terms, or personal data beyond your email.

How long does cashback take?

Affiliate networks typically confirm orders within 24-72 hours. After confirmation, your balance is updated immediately. Withdrawals are processed within 1 business day.

What if I return the item?

If the order is refunded or returned, the affiliate network reverses the commission. Your cashback will be deducted from your balance. This is standard across all cashback services.

Can I use cheapSkate with other cashback extensions?

You can, but we recommend using cheapSkate alone for the best experience. Running multiple cashback extensions can cause conflicts at checkout and may reduce your total savings. cheapSkate is designed to give you the highest possible share — 50% of every commission.

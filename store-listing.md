## Detailed Description

cheapSkate automatically finds coupon codes and cashback offers when you shop online. Just shop normally — we do the rest.

### How it works
1. **Install** the extension — it's free, no signup required to start
2. **Shop as usual** — visit any of 17+ supported stores
3. **At checkout** — cheapSkate shows coupon codes and cashback offers
4. **Apply & save** — click to auto-fill the coupon code and activate your cashback
5. **Get paid** — you keep 50% of the affiliate commission, paid out in crypto

### What you get
- **Coupon codes** auto-applied at checkout — save 5-20% instantly
- **Cashback** on every purchase — up to 10% on most orders
- **50% commission split** — we share the affiliate revenue 50/50
- **Free withdrawal** — no fees, no minimums (except $5 minimum)
- **Referral bonus** — earn 10% of your friends' cashback

### Supported stores
Amazon, eBay, Nike, Best Buy, Walmart, Target, Home Depot, Lowe's, Dell, Apple, Booking.com, Expedia, DoorDash, Uber Eats, NordVPN, Skillshare, and more added weekly.

### Privacy & security
- cheapSkate never reads your passwords, bank details, or personal messages
- We only detect which store you're on and when you're at checkout
- No data sold to third parties — ever
- Full source available on GitHub for transparency

### Permissions explained
- `storage` — saves your account and preferences locally
- `alarms` — periodically refreshes offers in the background
- `tabs` — opens the withdrawal page when you request it
- Host permissions — only to fetch offers from our API server

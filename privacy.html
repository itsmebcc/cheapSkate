Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. What We Collect

cheapSkate collects the minimum data needed to operate the cashback service:

  • Email address — When you create an account, we store your email for login and payout notifications.
  • Visited domains — The extension checks which online stores you visit to match against our offer database. This data is processed locally in your browser and not sent to our servers unless you choose to apply an offer.
  • Conversion data — When you complete a purchase through cheapSkate, we receive order amount, merchant, and commission details from the affiliate network. This is used solely to calculate your cashback.
  • User ID — A unique identifier assigned at registration, used to track your balance and conversions.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • Full browsing history
  • Search queries or search history
  • Personal details beyond email (name, address, phone)
  • Payment information (withdrawals use crypto addresses you provide)
  • Content of your communications or messages

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To match offers with the stores you visit
  • To calculate and credit your cashback balance
  • To process withdrawals
  • To improve our offer database and extension

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on our servers (encrypted at rest). We use industry-standard security practices. Email addresses and user IDs are stored in a SQLite database. Conversion records are retained for payout reconciliation purposes.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. The only third-party data sharing is with affiliate networks when reporting a conversion — they receive order confirmation details (order ID, amount, merchant) necessary to validate the commission. These affiliate networks have their own privacy policies.

6. Extension Permissions

The cheapSkate browser extension requests the following permissions. Chrome Web Store requires us to explain each one:

  • Storage — Saves your user ID, preferences (enabled/disabled, cooldown period), and locally cached offers so the extension works offline. No browsing history or personal data is stored in extension storage beyond what's listed here.
  • Alarms — Periodically refreshes the offer database in the background (every 30 minutes). No data is collected or transmitted beyond fetching updated offers from our API.
  • Tabs — Detects the current URL to check for matching offers and coupons. Used only to determine which store you're on. We never read the content of your tabs.
  • Host permissions — Communicates with our API server at cheapskate.gg to fetch offers and report conversions. Also redirects through affiliate links when you choose to apply an offer. We never modify or intercept your communications with other websites.

The extension processes visited domains locally in your browser — your browsing activity is not sent to our servers unless you choose to apply an offer and complete a purchase. The extension does not read passwords, payment details, messages, or any page content beyond detecting the current store domain.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Specifically:

  • Account data (email, user ID, referral code) — until you request deletion
  • Conversion records — retained for payout reconciliation and fraud prevention (typically 12 months after last activity)
  • Referral data — retained to calculate ongoing referral commissions

You can request deletion at any time by emailing privacy@cheapskate.gg. We will delete your personal data within 30 days.

8. Referral Data

When you use a referral link to sign up, we record which user referred you. This is used to calculate referral commissions (10% of earnings). Referral data is not shared with third parties.

9. Cookies on Our Website

Our marketing site (cheapskate.gg) uses minimal cookies:

  • Waitlist cookie — Remembers that you joined the waitlist so we don't show the form again
  • Analytics — We may use privacy-focused analytics (e.g., Plausible) that do not track individual users

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party marketing cookies.

10. Your Rights (GDPR / CCPA)

If you are located in the EU or California, you have the following rights:

  • Right to access — Request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Right to deletion — Request erasure of your personal data
  • Right to portability — Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object — Object to specific processing activities

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@cheapskate.gg.

11. Children's Privacy

cheapSkate is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided personal data, contact us and we will delete it.

12. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy as the service evolves. Continued use of cheapSkate after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. Material changes will be notified via email or extension update notes.

13. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@cheapskate.gg.

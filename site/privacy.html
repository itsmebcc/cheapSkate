Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

1. What We Collect

cheapSkate collects the minimum data needed to operate the cashback service:

  • Email address — When you create an account, we store your email for login and payout notifications.
  • Visited domains — The extension checks which online stores you visit to match against our offer database. This data is processed locally in your browser and not sent to our servers unless you choose to apply an offer.
  • Conversion data — When you complete a purchase through cheapSkate, we receive order amount, merchant, and commission details from the affiliate network. This is used solely to calculate your cashback.
  • User ID — A unique identifier assigned at registration, used to track your balance and conversions.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • Full browsing history
  • Search queries or search history
  • Personal details beyond email (name, address, phone)
  • Payment information (withdrawals use crypto addresses you provide)
  • Content of your communications or messages

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To match offers with the stores you visit
  • To calculate and credit your cashback balance
  • To process withdrawals
  • To improve our offer database and extension

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on our servers (encrypted at rest). We use industry-standard security practices. Email addresses and user IDs are stored in a SQLite database. Conversion records are retained for payout reconciliation purposes.

You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing us.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. The only third-party data sharing is with affiliate networks when reporting a conversion — they receive order confirmation details (order ID, amount, merchant) necessary to validate the commission. These affiliate networks have their own privacy policies.

6. Extension Permissions

The cheapSkate browser extension requests the following permissions:

  • Storage — To cache offers and your user ID locally
  • Tabs — To detect the current URL and check for offers
  • Host permissions — To communicate with our API server and redirect through affiliate links

The extension does not read or modify the content of pages beyond detecting the current domain and displaying our popup overlay.

7. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy as the service evolves. Continued use of cheapSkate after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at privacy@cheapskate.gg.
